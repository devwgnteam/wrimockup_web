Our Team

Meet the Woodward Renovations Team

Our team is dedicated to providing high-quality service and attention to detail in every project. With years of experience in home renovation and property management, we are committed to transforming your vision into reality.

Michael Woodward Sr.

Founder & CEO

Michael Woodward Sr. began his interest in real estate renovations as a youth working with his grandfather, father and great uncle doing handyman and maintenance services. He purchased his first property and renovated it himself at just 20 years old. Utilizing all of his prior knowledge and experience in real estate, Michael turned a part time residual income opportunity into a full time business when he started Woodward Property Group. Today, Woodward Property Group is a full service home improvement and property management company that has been providing exceptional home renovation and property management services since 2012.
