Welcome to Woodward Renovations Inc., a home renovation and property management service provider. We offer a wide range of services and specialize in home renovations. Our services include timber framing basements, kitchen and bathroom renovations, interior and exterior painting, siding, deck remodels, kitchen and bathroom design and customization, schools, flooring, and exterior restoration. As a husband and wife-owned business, we are proud to say that we are A+ rated by the Better Business Bureau and authorized to be licensed and insured.

No job is too large or small; we make sure to provide personalized and detailed servicing to each customer. With our expertise, we are sure to make your home into the dream you have always wanted.

We understand that remodeling projects can be stressful, which is why we offer our services with confidence and enthusiasm for each job. We make sure that your project runs smoothly and that you are always kept as informed as possible. If you are in the process of buying or selling a property and need property management, don't hesitate to contact Woodward Renovations today so that we can discuss the best solution for you.

If you would like to take on a renovation project yourself, our team is ready to answer any questions that you may have and provide you with the best service possible. We invite you to get in touch with Woodward Renovations and make your dream home come true.

We invite you to take our quote-and-sketch questionnaire so that we can create the best plan for you and your property. Let us transform your property into the dream you have always wanted. For more information, please contact us at (678) 439-8003.

Our story begins with a dedication to excellence and a vision for growth. Our founder, Michael Woodward Sr., was raised in a family dedicated to real estate renovations, honing his skills while working alongside his grandfather, father, and great uncle. His real estate journey took a significant turn when he purchased and renovated his first property at the age of 20, creating a full-time income opportunity out of a passion project.

In the present day, Woodward Renovations Inc. thrives on its deep expertise in the home improvement and property management industries. Its team is dedicated to providing high-quality service and attention to detail in every project. Each service provided, such as basement renovation, kitchen and bathroom renovations, deck replacement, exterior and interior painting, flooring, and comprehensive property management, is delivered with a commitment to excellence.

Woodward Renovations Inc has established multiple partnerships throughout the metro Atlanta area that showcase its leadership in the property management and renovation industries. The company's A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau provides a testament to its reliability, credibility, and commitment to serving its customers on every project. We invite you to explore our services and experience the difference in quality, customer satisfaction, and attention to detail.
